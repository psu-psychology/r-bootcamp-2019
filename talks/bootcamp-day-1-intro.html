The storRy of our R

  • What is R?
    • A programming language, written by and for statisticians & data scientists
  • Why is it called R?
    • TLDR: its the free, open-source version of a commercial program called S developed in the 1970s at Bell Labs
    • Bell Labs is also the home of C, and the Unix operating system

The storRy of R

  • Features of R
    • Written in C, Fortran, and R
    • 20th most popular programming language
    • Base R (now 3.6.1) + thousands of packages
    • Object-oriented
